🎯 QUICK START GUIDE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 1: INITIAL SETUP (10 minutes)
───────────────────────────────────
1. Open: baghoor-enhanced-dashboard.html
2. Click "+ Add Customer"
3. Enter name, contact, and BOTH rates:
   → Petrol Rate: Rs. 253
   → HSD Rate: Rs. 245
4. Repeat for all customers
5. Click "+ Add Supplier"
6. Enter supplier details with their rates
7. Repeat for all suppliers

STEP 2: DAILY TRANSACTIONS (30 seconds each)
─────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Select "Purchase" or "Sale"
2. Pick customer/supplier (rates auto-load!)
3. Pick product (Petrol/HSD)
4. System shows their rate automatically
5. Enter quantity
6. Enter discount (if any)
7. System calculates total
8. Click Save!

STEP 3: EVERY 15 DAYS - RATE UPDATE (5 minutes)
────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Click "💱 Update Rates" button
2. See all customers and suppliers in one screen
3. Update all rates:
   → Old: 253 → New: 257 (just type new number)
4. Click "Save All Rates"
5. Done! New rates now apply automatically

SCENARIO: Sale to Ahmed Traders with discount

Before (Old Way):
─────────────────
❌ Look up Ahmed's rate manually
❌ Remember it's Rs. 253
❌ Customer wants 3 Rs discount
❌ Calculate: 253 - 3 = 250 (on calculator)
❌ Calculate total: 1000 × 250 = 250,000 (on calculator)
❌ Enter everything manually
⏱️  Time: 2-3 minutes

After (New Way):
────────────────
✅ Select "Sale to Customer"
✅ Select "Ahmed Traders" → Rate loads automatically (253)
✅ Enter quantity: 1000
✅ Enter discount: 3
✅ System shows: Final Rate: 250, Total: 250,000
✅ Click Save
⏱️  Time: 30 seconds
